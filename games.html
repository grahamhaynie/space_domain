ECE 4525 - Video Game Design and Engineering

This class was focused on the design and developement of Video Games. The focus was not on the graphics engine, for that Processing was used. Projects were developed weekly, with each week introducing new concepts to be applied. For specific concepts, see the individual projects. With the exception of the 8th project, all projects are 2-Dimensional games. My focus was less on the graphics than the gameplay, so please excuse my poor artistic abilities. I would recommend checking out the Final Project if you only check one out - as it was a several week long project and is much more refined. To play a game, click on the thumbnail.

Note: When playing games, make sure you change your broswer size so that you do not have a scroll bar. You'll need to play them with a keyboard, mobile won't work.
