Project 5 - Pirrrate Battle

Type: Game

Concepts: Modelling Intelligent Behavior

Description:

The goal of this project was to crate a dodge-ball game with intelligent NPCs that are smart enough to dodge the balls from the player. The player is set at the bottom of the screen, and can shoot balls to try to prevent adversaries from making it across the screen. Adversaries will attempt to dodge incoming balls, but can be outsmarted.

Gameplay:

To begin play, read the instructions and click in the game's screen. Rotate the cannon with the left/right arrow keys, and use the up arrow to shoot a cannonball. Try to shoot the oncoming ships, as once a single ship reaches the right side of the screen you lose. Each ship must be hit 3 times before it is defeated. Certain ships will give you boosts or inhibit you when defeated.
