Project 4 - Monkeys and Tomatoes

Type: Game

Concepts: Linear Physics, Scrolling tile-map

Description:

The objective of this project was to create a game of the classic 'platformer' genre. That means that the player views their character in 2-dimensional space. The player can move left/right, jump up and fall down, as well as perform a special action - in this case drop a bouncing ball. They can jump between platforms, and try to do so without falling and while avoiding adversaries.

Gameplay:

To begin, read the instructions and press anywhere in the game screen to begin. Move the deer left/right with the left/right arrow keys. Jump with the up key, and drop a tomato with the down key. Try to stay on the green/brown platforms, if you fall out of the map you will lose. Move around the map and collect all of the bananas. Try to avoid the monkeys (who will chase you when near). To defeat the monkeys, drop a tomato on them from above. You can see how tomatoes many you have left on the top of the screen, as well as the number of monkeys left and bananas collected.
